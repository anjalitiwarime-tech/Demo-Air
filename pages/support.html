FAQ

Answers before you fly.

The most common questions are grouped around bookings, changes, baggage, and payments.

Use Manage Trip with your booking reference and last name. Eligible fares can change travel dates, seats, and add-ons before departure.
Cabin baggage is included on most fares. Checked baggage depends on route, cabin, and fare family and is shown before checkout.
Refund eligibility depends on fare rules. The manage-trip area can show refundable amounts, fees, and travel credit options.
Send a support request with your booking reference at least 48 hours before departure so the airport team can prepare.
